--key findings of the project:

Revenue & Orders

1. Total completed revenue comes from 17 out of 20 orders 
 (3 were cancelled/returned/pending), meaning an 85% order  completion rate

2. 3 orders failed — 2 cancellations (orders 1003, 1013) and 1 return (order 1009), giving a 15% failure rate
3. Smartwatch Pro (₹8,999) and Air Fryer (₹5,500) are the highest unit-price products, so even single-unit sales from these drive revenue significantly

Top Products are 

1. Wireless Headphones appears in 3 separate orders (1001, 1005, 1016) — the most reordered product in the dataset, making it the clear bestseller by frequency
2. Smartwatch Pro generates the highest revenue per order due to its ₹8,999 price point
3. Resistance Bands sold 3 units in one order — highest single-order quantity

Customer Behaviour

1. Aarav Mehta (customer 1) is the most active customer — placed 3 orders (Jan, May, Sep 2023), making him the only customer with that frequency
2. Karan Joshi and Vikram Nair are repeat buyers with 2 completed orders each
3. Ananya Bose (customer 6) has a pending order that was never completed — activation risk
4. Customers 9 (Arjun Singh) only has a cancelled order — effectively a zero-revenue customer despite signing up

City Performance

1. Mumbai has the highest order volume (Aarav Mehta alone has 3 orders), likely topping city revenue
2. Hyderabad has 2 active customers (Karan Joshi + Deepika Rao), both with completed orders

Product Ratings

1. Smartwatch Pro has mixed reviews — rated 2 by one customer and 5 by another, giving it the most polarising reception
2. Wireless Headphones and Mechanical Keyboard both received 4–5 star ratings — strong, consistent satisfaction
USB-C Hub received a 3 — the only mid-tier rated Electronics product

At-Risk Products

Air Fryer has only 25 units in stock and no reviews yet — high price, zero social proof, low inventory
Non-Stick Cookware Set has only 30 units — Home & Kitchen category is thin on stock overall
